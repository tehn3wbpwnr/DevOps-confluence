Conditions Needed to Implement & Further Research needed

Goal:

  • Automatically deploy code changes to production, quickly delivering new features and improvements to users.

Purpose:

  • Accelerate customer feedback loop and minimize release cycles, allowing developers to focus on coding and immediate delivery of work to users.

Conditions

Continuous Monitoring

  • Must quickly identify the impact of new deployments on users and be able to respond rapidly to any issues.

Rollback Strategy

  • Must have a rollback strategy to quickly revert to the previous version in case of issues after deployment.

Feedback Loop

  • Must analyze user behavior data to quickly identify problems and implement necessary improvements swiftly.
